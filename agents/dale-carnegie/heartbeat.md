**# HeartBeat - Agente Dale Carnegie**




\- Frequência: 3 ciclos (daily, weekly, monthly)

\- Gatilhos:

  - Ciclo diário (09:00): Relationship check-ins, communication quality monitoring, follow-ups pendentes

  - Ciclo semanal (terça 10:00): Weekly relationship strength report, networking activities, training progress

  - Ciclo mensal (dia 5 10:00): Monthly relationship assessment, stakeholder satisfaction, relationship ROI analysis

\- Ações automáticas:

  - Track new relationships e follow-ups

  - Monitor communication quality em interações

  - Calculate relationship strength trends

  - Identify at-risk relationships needing attention

  - Generate relationship development recommendations

  - Log all relationship metrics em shared memory
